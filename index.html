Why It Sticks

Built to feel useful first, unforgettable second.

The best upcycled products do not ask customers to compromise. They solve a real need, look intentional in a room, and only then reveal the material story behind them.

That is the core idea here: broken nails from construction waste are cleaned, sorted, bent, forged, grouped, and finished into objects that add structure, texture, and character to everyday spaces.
